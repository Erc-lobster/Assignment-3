Caramilk

General

Caramilk chocolate bars are made by Cadbury since 1968, they are milk chocolate bars with caramel inside of them. Fun fact the Caramilk bars in Canada are different to the ones in Europe. Caramilk chocolate bars have had some different Flavours throughout its history although the most common ones are the regular chocolate bar and salted caramel
